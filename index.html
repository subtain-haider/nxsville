Features

Special Features Thats Make You Happy And Cheer Up!

Parking Management System

Our Parking Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to simplify and optimize every aspect of parking operations. From managing dynamic rules and tariffs to handling permits and tickets, the system offers unparalleled flexibility and control. Seamlessly integrated with payment processing and fine management, it ensures smooth financial operations while delivering a user-friendly experience. Additionally, the system provides real-time parking information services, enabling users to easily find available spaces and access essential updates.

Smart Parking Lots

From barrier to barrier-less access, we simplify parking for on-street, off-street, and multi-storey facilities, ensuring smooth entry, exit, and management processes.

Smart Parking Inspection:

Powered by cutting-edge smart scanning vehicles and user-friendly mobile apps, our solution ensures accurate, efficient enforcement while reducing operational overhead.

Smart Tolling Gates

Equipped with state-of-the-art sensors, our gates support both tag-based tolling through RFID and DSRC readers, as well as vision-based tolling using highly accurate ANPR cameras. This ensures seamless vehicle identification and efficient toll collection, minimizing traffic delays.

Tolling Management Platform

At the core of our solution is a powerful back-office system that processes tolling transactions with precision. Leveraging a dynamic rules engine, the platform manages payments, permits, and fines, offering customized configurations to meet the unique needs of each client.

Fleet Management System

A comprehensive platform designed to monitor and track fleet performance in real-time. With advanced tools for tracking, dispatching, and route optimization, this system simplifies operations, minimizes fuel consumption, and ensures timely delivery of services.
